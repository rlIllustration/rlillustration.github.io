Cabinet of Curiosities- Cryptids

Summer University Project based on the Cabinet of curiosities. Mine is focusing on the study of Myths and Legends, Cryptology.

The Black and white photography was made on a pinhole camera and developed in my bathroom. All the items you see where also created to be photographed.

Created using a mix of Photography, Collage, Painting and printmaking.
